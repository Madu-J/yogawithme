About


Get down on the mat and stretch your muscles.

Yoga-with-me started 3 year ago through years of personal practices now metamorphosed into an online class for our community collective well-being and where everyone can join and take advantage of our service.


I like to inspire others to a healthy and fun way to take good care of their body, soul and mind. The good feeling that I get and the result I get from Yoga practices I am sure there are are plenty of people who would like to feel same way. We have yoga class for levels our sections includes stretching, downward facing, bridge pose, seated halfway-spinal twist, meditation and many more.

Get down on the mat and stretch your muscles. Relax and find your way back to peace of mind with our online training remedies for a healthy living


I use what I practice to educate others. Note, this website is only for educational purposes and for sixteen plus.
